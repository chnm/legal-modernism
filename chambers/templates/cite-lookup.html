Citation Lookup

Look up a single citation by its UUID. Each citation in the database was detected by the cite-detector-moml program, which extracted raw citation strings from the OCR text of pages in the Making of Modern Law (MOML) treatise collection. Every detected citation is assigned a UUID stored in moml_citations.citations_unlinked. The detail page shows the raw detected string, the result of the citation linking pipeline (cleaned, normalized, and linked forms), any matched case from the Caselaw Access Project, Code Reporter, or English Reports databases, and the full OCR text of the MOML page where the citation was found.

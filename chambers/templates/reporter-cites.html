{{.Reporter}}

This page shows up to 10,000 raw citations from moml_citations.citations_unlinked whose reporter_abbr maps to the standard reporter “{{.Reporter}}” via the legalhist.reporters_citation_to_cap whitelist. The displayed text is the raw column: the exact citation string as it was detected in the MOML OCR text. Each citation is color-coded by its linking status from moml_citations.citation_links: green means the citation was successfully linked to a case (status starts with linked); red means no matching case was found (skipped_no_match); blue means the citation was classified as junk or a statute (skipped_junk or skipped_statute); yellow means the citation has not yet been processed by the linker. Click any citation to see its full detail page.

Show SQL query 
SELECT cu.id, cu.raw, cl.status
FROM moml_citations.citations_unlinked cu
JOIN legalhist.reporters_citation_to_cap wl
  ON cu.reporter_abbr = wl.reporter_found
LEFT JOIN moml_citations.citation_links cl
  ON cl.citation_id = cu.id
WHERE wl.reporter_standard = '{{.Reporter}}'
ORDER BY cu.id
LIMIT 10000
